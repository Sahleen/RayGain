Our Core Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy treatments tailored to your needs

🦴

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Treatment for muscle, joint, bone, tendon, or ligament issues including back pain, sprains, and arthritis.

🏥

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Recovery support after surgeries like knee replacement, rotator cuff repair, and spinal surgery.

⚽

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Prevention and recovery from athletic injuries including ACL tears, tennis elbow, and shin splints.

🧠

Neurological Physiotherapy

Treatment for conditions like stroke, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and spinal cord injuries.

💪

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Focus on bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles often after fractures, joint replacements, or trauma.

✋

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, manipulation, and soft tissue massage.

💊

Pain Management

Non-drug therapies to manage chronic pain including neck pain, fibromyalgia, and sciatica.
